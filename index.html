Research interests

There are a variety of intracellular fluxes of molecules and information including signaling pathways, translation, and metabolic reactions.

I aim to quantitatively understand cellular adaptation to environmental stimuli using both computational and experimental approaches.

My research questions include but not limited to

  • How could we measure and quantify the fluxes?
  • How cells encode or sense the fluxes in measurable quantities?
  • How cells decode and transmit information on the fluxes to adaptive responses?

Since I have changed research fields several times, I am still in the process of narrowing down my interests and coming up with specific research topics that stimulate my curiosity and worth devoting my academic life to.
